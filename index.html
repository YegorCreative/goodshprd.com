Our Story

Good Shepherd began in Minnesota with a simple belief: clothing should last, tell stories, and honor the hands that made it. We search for vintage pieces with character and restore them with care—breathing new life into garments that deserve a second chapter.

Each item we restore is cleaned, repaired, and prepared to be worn again. We believe in slowing down, choosing quality, and celebrating the craft of making things that endure.
